Welcome to the Premium Signal Bot Setup

This guide will walk you through the essential steps to start using the Premium Signal Bot. Follow each section carefully to properly configure your MetaTrader 5 platform and broker account.

Installing MetaTrader 5

Step 1: Download MetaTrader 5 from the official website.

Step 2: Run the installer and complete the setup.

Step 3: Launch MetaTrader 5 and log in or create a new trading account.

MetaTrader 5 Configuration

Step 1: In MetaTrader 5, go to Tools > Options > Expert Advisors.

Step 2: Check "Allow Algorithmic Trading".

Step 3: Check "Allow WebRequest for listed URL".

Step 4: Add the following URL:

https:/iucapitalfund.com/

Step 5: Click OK to save changes.

Attach the Expert Advisor to a Chart

Step 1: Move the downloaded Expert Advisor to the MQL5/Experts directory in your MT5 installation.

Step 2: Open any chart from the Market Watch panel.

Step 3: Open the navigator and drag the Expert Advisor onto the chart. A configuration window will appear.

Step 4: Under the Inputs tab, enter the email and password you used to register.

Step 5: Set your risk percentage — the portion of your account balance to be risked per trade.

Step 6: Click OK to activate the bot.

Troubleshooting

If you experience any issues during the setup process, here are a few common solutions:

  • Bot Not Executing Trades: Double-check that algorithmic trading is enabled in MetaTrader 5 under Tools > Options > Expert Advisors.
  • WebRequest Not Working: Ensure you have added the correct server URL https:/iucapitalfund.com/ to the allowed list under the Expert Advisors tab.
  • Invalid Login: Make sure you entered the correct email and password in the bot’s Inputs tab when attaching it to the chart.
  • If you are sure you are entering the correct credentials, but it is failing authentication, ensure the address has been added to the web requests correctly.
  • Bot is Disabled(greyed out): Make sure the AutoTrading button in MetaTrader 5 is turned ON (green).
  • Still Stuck? Reach out to our support team through the Discord community.
